Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in, we collect your email address and display name through Firebase Authentication. We also store your favorites, settings, recently played games, and any suggestions you submit.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide site features — saving your favorites and settings, syncing them across sessions, and personalizing your experience.

3. Storage

Account and preference data is stored using Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore). Their handling of data is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third parties except as required to operate the service (e.g. Firebase).

5. Your Choices

You may stop using the site and request deletion of your account data by contacting the administrators.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the site means you accept the updated policy.
